Green Party rejects racism, discrimination and hate speech

I have always stood against racism. Sadly in the 2024 elections, being vocally anti-racist is more important than ever.

I have pledged to
  • Send a consistent and clear message that I reject racism, discrimination and hate speech.
  • Treat people of all faiths and of none with respect.
  • Condemn any campaign that may incite racism.
  • Use appropriate and inclusive language when referring to people of different ethnic and religious backgrounds, and of different sexual orientations.
